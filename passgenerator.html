Password Generator

Create Strong and Unique Passwords Effortlessly

PassGuard's Password Generator allows you to quickly and easily generate strong, unique passwords for all your accounts. Avoid weak or reused passwords by letting PassGuard create a password tailored to your needs.

Why Use the Password Generator?

  • Strong Security: Ensure your accounts are protected with random, complex passwords that are difficult to crack.
  • Customizable Length: Choose the length of your password depending on the requirements of the site or your personal preference.
  • Unique for Every Account: Generate a different password for every site, reducing the risk of compromised accounts.
  • One-Click Generation: Generate strong passwords with just one click and store them securely in your PassGuard vault.

How to Generate a Strong Password?

With PassGuard's Password Generator, you can specify the length of your password and generate a random, secure password instantly. Follow these steps:

  • Select your desired password length using the number input field below.
  • Click the "Generate Password" button.
  • Copy the generated password and use it for your accounts. You can store it directly in your PassGuard vault for easy access later.

Additional Features

PassGuard's Password Generator can be accessed via our browser extension, enabling you to create and autofill strong passwords directly in your browser without leaving the site. The extension supports password generation on the go, allowing you to strengthen your online security across all platforms.

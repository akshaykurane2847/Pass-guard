  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Enable 2FA for an extra layer of security. This ensures that only you can access your vault, even if your password is compromised.

  • Data Breach Alerts: We monitor known data breaches and alert you if any of your stored credentials are compromised, allowing you to act swiftly.
  • Regular Audits: Our system undergoes regular security audits to ensure it stays ahead of potential threats.

Getting Started with PassGuard

Getting started with PassGuard is easy and only takes a few steps:

  1. Create an Account: Sign up for an account with your email and a strong master password.
  2. Set Up 2FA: For added security, we highly recommend enabling two-factor authentication during the setup process.
  3. Import Your Passwords: You can import your existing passwords from other password managers, or manually enter them into your PassGuard vault.
  4. Download the App: Install PassGuard on your desktop, mobile, or tablet to access your passwords from anywhere.
  5. Start Using Autofill: Once set up, PassGuard will autofill your login credentials on websites and apps, making your login process seamless and secure.

Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is PassGuard?

PassGuard uses end-to-end encryption to protect your data, ensuring that only you have access to your passwords. Additionally, features like two-factor authentication and breach alerts provide extra layers of security.

Can I access PassGuard on multiple devices?

Yes! PassGuard allows you to sync your passwords across all your devices, including desktops, mobile phones, and tablets, so you can access your passwords wherever you are.

What happens if I forget my master password?

Since we use zero-knowledge encryption, we cannot reset your master password. However, you can set up recovery methods like an emergency contact or recovery keys when you create your account.

How does the password generator work?

Our password generator creates complex and unique passwords for each account, making it difficult for hackers to guess. You can customize the length and type of characters used in the password to suit your needs.
